Our Story

Because our primary product is BQSAFEWASH Imagine having a healthy baby and having to be admitted back to the hospital few days after child birth due to escalated skin irritation(Dermatitis) as a result of using poorly formulated laundry detergent. This was our founder's experience with her son in 2019.
As a mother, who has witnessed first hand the dangers of using regular laundry detergent for her baby, and also the several bitter complaints of parents on motherhood platforms, she was moved to research as a pharmacologist, and this lead to the innovation of BQSAFEWASH, a liquid laundry detergent specifically formulated for babies and children of African decent.
As we grew, we saw the need for equally efficient and safe liquid detergent for everyday use, and we got to work, formulating our highly effective anti-bacterial BQ Hand Wash to take care of your hands and our deep grease cutting BQ Utensil Wash, to take care of your dishes as your kitchen cleaning expert.
All our products are NAFDAC CERTIFIED and DERMATOLOGIST TESTED Who We Are BQ GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LIMITED is a Nafdac approved, innovative detergent company, we use non-toxic materials in our products which makes them safe for the entire family.
We take pride in providing high-value products that ensure your safety and satisfaction Overtime, we have identified as a family oriented brand and we show this in our safety-conscious standards and in how we work as a team. We believe in consistently showing up with the highest standards in our product delivery.
